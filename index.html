Who we are

A garden, a community

La Tercera Garden is a community garden in Petaluma, California, re-established in 2024 after many years of abandonment. A collaborative project of the City of Petaluma, it brings local residents together to grow their own produce in a healthy, shared environment.

Our plots grow everything from salad greens and garlic to strawberries, artichokes, and flowers.

We meet informally throughout the season and gather for community breakfasts, workdays, and summer events. New members are welcome — spots open periodically from our waiting list.
