Who we are

A garden, a community

La Tercera Garden is a community garden in Petaluma, California, established in 2024. We are a small, close-knit group of gardeners sharing raised beds, tools, knowledge, and the occasional croissant.

Our plots grow everything from salad greens and garlic to strawberries, artichokes, and flowers. The garden is a public space managed in partnership with the City of Petaluma, open to anyone who wants to put their hands in the soil.

We meet informally throughout the season and gather for community breakfasts, workdays, and summer events. New members are welcome — spots open periodically from our waiting list.
